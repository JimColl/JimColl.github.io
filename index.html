About Me

Hi. My name is Jim Coll and I’m a geospatial hydroscience developer at the University of Alabama for the Office of Water Prediction. My skill set revolves broadly around the intersection of GIScience and Hydrology with a flair towards FOSS (Free and Open Source Software). My masters work was in quantifying and visualizing the global trend in snow cover. My dissertation seeks to demonstrate how small UAS (drones) can be used to measure bathymetry and discharge at hyper-resolution scales.
