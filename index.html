Biography

Hi. My name is Jim Coll, I am a PhD Candidate in Geography at the University of Kansas.

My skillset revolves broadly around the intersection of GIScience, Remote Sensing, and Hydrology with a flair towards the FOSS(Free and Open Source Software). My masters work was in quantifying and visualizing the global trend in snow cover. My dissertation seeks to demonstrate how small UAS (Drones) can be used to measure bathymetry and discharge at hyper-resolution scales. Intense dislikes include temperatures over 70o, seafood, but I love flying drones, dogs, and longboarding (the snowboarding in Kansas is sub-par. Thanks for visiting, and if you have any questions feel free to reach out!
