Week 1

Syllabus day

As noted in blackboard, I will run the class off of this website but all submissions will be through blackboard. High points of syllabus day:

  • The lab is a huge part of this class, don’t blow it off.
  • Using ArcMap at least 2 times a week will greatly increase your learning outcomes.
  • This class counts towards the Undergraduate certificate in GIS, which in turn counts as 5 of the 30 points necessary for a GISP, (Your degree counts towards another 20). I obviously recommend it.
  • There will be a final project and presentation, and two exams. Although not strictly cumulative, this is all foundational…
  • There will be a practical portion on either/or/both the midterm and final.
